The Prompt Blueprint

Key Ingredients

  • ROLE: Who is the AI? (e.g., "You are a Chemist")
  • TASK: What is the specific action? (e.g., "Explain...")
  • CONTEXT: Who is it for? (e.g., "For 8th graders")
  • CONSTRAINTS: Length, tone, or rules? (e.g., "No jargon")
  • OUTPUT: What format? (e.g., "A 3-column table")

Objective-Based Templates

Academic

Academic & Study

Explain Concept

"Explain [concept] like I'm [age]. Use an analogy and a real-world example."

Step-by-Step Guide

"Show me how to solve [problem] step-by-step. Explain the logic of each step."

Improve Vocabulary

"Replace common words in this [paragraph] with more academic, precise terms."

Writing

Writing & Communication

Rewrite / Summarize

"Summarize [text] for [audience] in [length]. Focus on [key points]."

Writing Assistant

"Act as an editor. Review my [essay/draft] for flow and clarity. Suggest 3 specific edits."

Professional Email

"Write a [tone] email to [recipient] regarding [subject]. Goal: [ask/decline/thank]."

Creative

Brainstorm & Fun

Entertainment / Games

"Create a text-based adventure game set in [setting]. Give me 3 choices per turn."

Gift Ideas

"Suggest 5 gift ideas for a [age/gender] who likes [interests]. Budget: $[amount]."

Brainstorming

"Generate 10 'wild' ideas for [project/theme]. No idea is too crazy. Format: Bullet list."

Language

Language Learning

Partner Roleplay

"Act as a native [language] speaker. Let's talk about [topic]. Ask one question at a time."

Level-Specific Essay

"Write a 200-word essay on [topic] using [CEFR Level] vocabulary in [language]."

Grammar & Punctuation

"Analyze this sentence for grammar errors. Explain why they are wrong and show the fix."

Tech

Tech & Coding

Generate Website

"Write a single HTML file with Tailwind CSS for a [type of site]. Include a hero and a footer."

Debug Code

"Identify the bug in this [language] code: [paste code]. Explain how to fix it."

Planning

Personal Plan

Study Schedule

"Create a [timeframe] study schedule for [subject]. Focus on [weak area]. Max 1 hour/day."

Diet & Workout

"Design a 3-day meal plan and workout for [goal]. Constraints: [allergies/no equipment]."

The "Bad Answer" Fixes

Force a Plan

"Outline your approach in 5 bullets first. Then answer."

Remove Ambiguity

"Ask me 3 clarifying questions before you generate the response."

Ask for Reasoning

"List your assumptions and explain 2 ways this could be wrong."
